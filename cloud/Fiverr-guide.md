## üöÄ **PART 1: ACCOUNT & PROFILE SETUP**

### **Step 1: Create Your Account**
1. Go to **fiverr.com**
2. Click **"Join"** (top right corner)
3. Sign up options:
   - Google account (fastest)
   - Email address
   - Apple ID
   - Facebook
4. Verify your email (check inbox for code)

### **Step 2: Profile Photo Requirements**
#### **DO:**
- Clear face photo (shoulders up)
- Good lighting (face a window)
- Smile naturally
- Plain/simple background
- Recent photo

#### **DON'T:**
- Group photos
- Sunglasses or hats
- Blurry or dark images
- Logos (unless you're a business)
- Filters that change your appearance

#### **Quick Tip:**
Take photo near a window during daytime. Use your phone front camera. Ask someone to take it, or use timer.

### **Step 3: Display Name**
- **Format:** FirstName + Last Initial
- **Good Examples:** AlexR, SarahM, JohnD, MikeWriter, EmmaContent
- **Avoid:** Random numbers (John12345), unprofessional names (CoolDude99), hard to remember names

### **Step 4: Professional Title (One-Liner)**
- Appears under your name
- Make it specific

#### **Examples:**
- **For Blog Writing:** `Blog Writer & Content Creator | SEO Articles`
- **For Product Descriptions:** `E-commerce Writer | Product Descriptions That Sell`
- **For YouTube Scripts:** `YouTube Script Writer | Engaging Video Content`
- **For General Content:** `Freelance Writer | Blogs, Articles & Web Content`
